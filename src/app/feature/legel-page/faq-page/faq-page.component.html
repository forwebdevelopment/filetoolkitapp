Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is FileMarketHub free?

Yes, all tools on FileMarketHub are 100% free to use.

2. Do you store my files?

No. All files are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after the operation.

3. What file types are supported?

PDF, Word (.docx), PowerPoint (.pptx), Images and more.

4. Can I use this on mobile?

Yes, FileMarketHub works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

5. Are my passwords safe?

All processing happens locally on the server for a few seconds only. We do not store passwords or documents.

6. Why does processing take time?

Large files, weak internet, or heavy compression may increase the processing time slightly.
